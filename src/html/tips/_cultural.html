Cultural Etiquette

  • Behavior and Attire

    Dress modestly, covering shoulders and knees.

    Wear light, breathable clothing suitable for the rainforest.

    Avoid flashy accessories that may offend local sensibilities.

    Behave respectfully; be mindful of noise levels and personal space.

  • Working with guides

    Follow their instructions for safety and cultural respect

    Engage actively; ask questions and show interest.

    Understanding local customs

    Respect traditional practices and rituals.

    Avoid intrusive behavior; ask permission before taking photos.

    Learn basic phrases in the local language.
