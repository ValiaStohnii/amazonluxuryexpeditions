Activities and Experiences

  • Wildlife viewing

    Experience the thrill of spotting exotic animals in their natural habitats, guided by experts who help you discover the Amazon's incredible biodiversity.

  • Birdwatching

    Enjoy the sight of colorful and rare bird species, using binoculars and expert guidance to identify and learn about these avian wonders.

  • Jungle treks

    Embark on guided walks through dense rainforest, uncovering hidden gems of nature and learning about the diverse plant and animal life.

  • Cultural encounters

    Engage with indigenous communities, learning about their traditions, lifestyle, and deep connection with the Amazon environment.
